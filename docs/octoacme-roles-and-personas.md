# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Translates business requirements into actionable items, bridging stakeholders and the project team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Facilitate requirements workshops
- Clarify requirements with stakeholders and developers
- Validate developed solutions meet business needs

### Goals
- Ensure clear, actionable, and testable requirements
- Minimize rework due to requirement misunderstandings

### Typical Communication
- Frequently interacts with stakeholders, developers, and testers via workshops, clarifications, and walkthroughs.

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Facilitates Agile ceremonies and ensures Agile best practices are followed.

### Responsibilities
- Organize and facilitate stand-ups, sprint planning, and retros
- Remove roadblocks for the team
- Coach team on process improvement

### Goals
- Maintain a healthy team process and pace
- Ensure impediments are swiftly handled

### Typical Communication
- Coordinates with all team members and product owner; runs agile meetings.

---

## Quality Assurance Lead

### Role Summary
Owns definition and enforcement of quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain test plans/cases
- Coordinate manual and automated testing
- Validate release readiness

### Goals
- Deliver defect-minimized releases
- Maintain focus on quality throughout the lifecycle

### Typical Communication
- Works with developers, product managers, and stakeholders on acceptance criteria.

---

## Stakeholder

### Role Summary
Provides business context and feedback, and formally approves milestones as needed.

### Responsibilities
- Review key deliverables
- Offer business feedback and approvals
- Communicate business priorities

### Goals
- Ensure project meets true business need

### Typical Communication
- Communicates periodically via reviews, demos, or check-ins.

----

## Solution Architect

### Role Summary
Defines technical architecture and alignment with business/technical goals.

### Responsibilities
- Design solution architecture and standards
- Validate designs with developers and stakeholders
- Ensure requirements are technically feasible

### Goals
- Support scalability, maintainability, and technical fit

### Typical Communication
- Works closely with developers, business analysts, and project managers.
